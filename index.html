Information

Your location is indicated by the blue magnifying glass.

The blue circle around the magnifying glass indicates, approximately, how accurate your location is - the wider the circle, the less accurate.

Clues around town are indicated by red flags.

In order to read the clue, tap on a red flag. If you are close enough (within 15 metres or so) when you tap on it the flag will turn green and the clue will be displayed. See if you can find the answer in the vicinity. Make a note and bring it back to the office with you.

Bear in mind your map location will update every few seconds so may not be 100% accurate - give it a couple of seconds to catch up when you think you are in the right area for the clue.

All answers to the clues are numbers which are displayed on or calculated from (with very simple maths!) structures/signs/objects of note in town.

All clues are located outside, you do not need to go into any buildings to find them.

Once activated tapping on a flag will show the clue again no matter how far away you are from it.

Clues will continue to be displayed on the screen until you close them (press the cross on the clue) or until you tap on another flag.

You can scroll round the map by dragging your finger across it.

You can zoom in on the map by putting two fingers on the screen and dragging them apart. Reverse this action to zoom out.

To re-centre the map on your location tap the "CENTRE" button at the top of the page.

If you zoom out too far simply tap the "ZOOM" button at the bottom of the page and it will reset the zoom, then tap "CENTRE" to centre on you again.

Finally, if you are really lucky you might come across some unexpected surprises - find as many clues as possible to collect the surprises!

Good luck and happy hunting!
